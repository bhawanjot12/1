Perfect Smoothie

Want the perfect recipe for a smoothie well you have come to the right place, this is a super simple recipe and takes only 5 mins.

1:

The first and most important step for a smoothie is selecting and cutting fruits and vegetables you want; it's all about that perfect combo!

2:

Put everything in a blender (with ice). That ice is going to give it a little more fresh taste, after that just blend away.

3:

Congrats your smoothie is almost done, for a final touch add a layer of whipped cream and mix with your straw or a spoon.
